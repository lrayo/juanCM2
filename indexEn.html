About Us

We are a company specialized in the management and analysis of information for decision-making, both in web and mobile environments, in which company decision-makers can monitor critical business variables and have relevant information available anywhere.
Our interdisciplinary team, with over 20 years of experience not only in information technology but also in organizational consulting focused on financial and process areas, gives us clarity to identify what our clients want to see and understand, and to present the information in the most practical and user-friendly way, facilitating the decision-making process.

Our Services

All our developments are supported by a powerful world-class BI platform, which guarantees scalability, security, agile and timely responses, and the ability to deliver precise information for each stakeholder.

Commercial Developments

Our developments allow for a 360-degree view that integrates financial, logistics, production, customer service, and sales areas. Additionally, our developments allow for complete mobile availability of information in real-time. They have transactionality, which allows for filling out forms, taking orders, taking and sending photos, signing, reading barcodes, and even conducting surveys.

Financial Developments

Our developments allow for the analysis of current and historical financial information, providing decision-makers with greater possibilities of assertiveness in decision-making.

Legal Developments

Our developments allow for the secure management of the Board of Directors' legal information, mobile, with or without a network connection, always seeking to condense all the required information so that interested parties have complete access to the information.

Special Developments

Our developments allow for the analysis of a large volume of information (Big Data), are customizable to the client's needs, and have great capacity for processing information in seconds.

iOS and Android Developments

Our iOS and Android development team has extensive experience in building high-quality, feature-rich, and scalable apps that meet the unique needs of our clients. From concept to deployment, we work closely with our clients to ensure that the final product exceeds their expectations and delivers a seamless user experience.

Cookie Policy

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. Cookies allow the website to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time.

2. What Cookies Do We Use?

On the Mega Deep Analytics SAS website, we use the following types of cookies:

2.1 Technical Cookies (Necessary)

These are essential for website operation and cannot be disabled. They are generally set in response to actions you take, such as setting privacy preferences, logging in, or completing forms.

  • Name: session_id
  • Purpose: Maintain user session
  • Duration: Session

2.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources to measure and improve our site's performance. They help us know which pages are most and least popular and see how visitors navigate the site.

  • Name: _ga, _gid (Google Analytics)
  • Purpose: Web traffic analysis
  • Duration: 2 years (_ga), 24 hours (_gid)

2.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or region) and provide enhanced and more personalized features.

  • Name: lang_preference
  • Purpose: Remember language preference
  • Duration: 1 year

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some external services we use may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: For web traffic analysis
  • Google Fonts: To load web fonts
  • Font Awesome: For site icons

4. How to Manage Cookies?

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies already on your device and configure most browsers to prevent them from being stored.

Cookie Management in Major Browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options > Privacy and security > Cookies and site data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Privacy, search and services > Cookies and site data

Note: If you block or delete cookies, some website functions may not work properly.
